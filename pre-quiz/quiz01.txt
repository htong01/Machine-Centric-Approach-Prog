# 7.
Why do switch/case statements use the keyword `break`, but if/else statements do not?
(string) ---
Switch/case statements need ‘break’ to end the current case. While if/else can end execution if not meet the requirement.

# 8.
What is the difference between a while and do-while loop (besides the fact that a do-while begins with the keyword `do`)?
(string) ---
While loop checks its conditional expression at the top of the loop, do-while loop checks it at the bottom.
